Full CCRC (Continuing Care Retirement Community) — can age in place through skilled nursing without moving. Need to call for exact assisted living + care level pricing and any entry fee.

## Reviews (Caring.com — 4.2/5, 72 reviews)

**Positives:** Food frequently praised as "gourmet" and "like a cruise ship" with steak nights — strong contrast to Sakura. Staff generally warm and attentive. Very clean, described as "five-star hotel" feel. Active programming: shopping trips, exercise, entertainment.

**Watch-outs:** Some reviews note food quality has declined over time and become more institutional. CCRC buy-in fees can be high — get details on entry fee. Staff consistency is mixed (some negative experiences). Limited outdoor access reported on some floors.
